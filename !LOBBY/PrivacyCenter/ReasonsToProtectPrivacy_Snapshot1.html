The importance of encryption

Encryption is an extremely important protocol on the internet. Encryption allows passwords and other important data from being stolen when going through. If encryption is lost, passwords can easily be stolen, personal info can easily be leaked, and there will be other massive consequences.

The harm of data collection

Data collection is becoming a common practice, and it is a very bad thing. When data is being collected, you become a product. You are sold to other companies for advertising. Your life becomes public, and if something gets leaked, your life can get ruined.

Small data collection, such as crash logs, and other info not related to your life, is fine, but when your entire life is for sale, it is a very bad thing.

Major companies and government branches that invade privacy and commit illegal data collection include: Google, Microsoft, Federal Bureau of Investigation (FBI), Central Intelligence Agency (CIA), National Security Assosication (NSA), Facebook (including Instagram and Whatsapp), Amazon, and hundreds more.

This doesn't have to be the future. You can easily make a difference by not using Google, Microsoft, or other services, but the government is harder to avoid, and still poses a major risk.
