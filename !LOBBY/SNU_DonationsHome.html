SNU Donations center

Welcome to the SNU donations center.

As SNU is open-source software, we only make money througb donations and advertising. If you are unable to donate money, you can still contribute and use the site in its entirety.

Every penny counts initiative

The every penny counts initiative is the plan for SNU to go to every length to make every cent that is donated to count. Donaters can come from all economical backgrounds, there may be some people who consider donating a dollar a lot, some others, even more.

The every penny counts plan makes it so that as much work goes into SNU for every cent that is donated.
