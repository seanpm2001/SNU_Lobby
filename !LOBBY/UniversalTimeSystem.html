The Universal Time System

SNU operates with a special time system known as the Universal Time System. It is designed to work on multiple planets, and also align with all regions on Earth.

The time zone is based on the time zone on the center of Earth () So in other areas, time will be negative or positive.

You can see content uploaded -8 hours ago. When seeing this, that means you are 8 hours away from the time zone that the content was uploaded.
